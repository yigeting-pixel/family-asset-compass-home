# Approval Flow

## Flow

```text
Advisor creates proposal
  ↓
Advisor submits approval request
  ↓
Supervisor / Risk reviews proposal
  ↓
Approve / Reject / Cancel
  ↓
Audit log records decision
  ↓
Client-facing report can be generated after approval
```

## Review Checklist

- Client risk level
- Product risk level
- Liquidity needs
- Concentration risk
- Industry exposure
- Manager concentration
- Holding overlap
- Compliance notes
- Disclaimers
